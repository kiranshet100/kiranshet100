### Currently building

**📈 Tradelix** — professional trading journal that fights for your discipline.
Live behavioral tilt detection (losing streaks, revenge sizing, rapid-fire entries), a composite Edge Score (profit factor × win rate × discipline × consistency), prop-firm risk-rule tracking, AI trade coaching, and a ⌘K command palette.
`Next.js 16` `React 19` `Prisma 7` `Turso` `Clerk` `Recharts`

**🔍 SeenByAI** — AI search visibility tracker.
Businesses lose customers they never see when ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity don't recommend them. SeenByAI measures that visibility and shows how to improve it.
`Next.js` `TypeScript` `LLM APIs`
