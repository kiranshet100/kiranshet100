**Tradelix** — a professional trading journal that actually fights for your discipline.
Live behavioral tilt detection (losing streaks, revenge sizing, rapid-fire entries), an Edge Score that fuses profit factor + win rate + discipline into one number, prop-firm risk rule tracking, AI trade coaching, and a ⌘K command palette. Next.js 16 · Prisma 7 · Turso · Clerk.

**SeenByAI** — AI search visibility tracker. Find out whether ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity recommend your business — and fix it when they don't.
